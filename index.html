About Us

We are Joe and Josh and we’re twins, so we’ve shared stuff our whole lives, and now here we are sharing a business. We both study biomedical engineering in the University of Florence, Italy. We somehow needed to get our minds off the books, so we both found a passion which could stimulate our creativity. Joe is a freelance self thought product 3D artist, who’s been passionate of softwares such as Blender since lockdown. Josh is also a freelancer self thought logo designer and brand identity. We like learning new things or exploring, in fact in our spare time we love creating music together by playing the piano and the drums, which has let us to we get along really well. We always make sure to understand what the brand desires to communicate when making our projects in order to communicate it through the logo or the project animations. We love building long term relationships, however if you need a logo or a 3d project of your product feel free to send us a message!
